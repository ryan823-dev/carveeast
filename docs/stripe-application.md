## 1. 业务描述文案

### 英文版本（推荐用这个）

**Business Description:**

CarveEast is a curated online platform connecting international art collectors with contemporary Chinese artists. We specialize in traditional Chinese art forms including seal engraving, calligraphy, ceramics, and ink painting.

**Business Model:**
- Direct sales of original artworks (one-time payments)
- Auction events with live bidding
- Commission-based artist representation
- Art advisory services for collectors

**Target Markets:**
- Primary: United States, United Kingdom, Europe
- Secondary: Australia, Canada, Singapore, Hong Kong
- Customer base: Art collectors, interior designers, cultural institutions

**Average Transaction Value:**
- Typical artwork: $500 - $5,000 USD
- Premium pieces: $5,000 - $20,000 USD
- Auction lots: $1,000 - $15,000 USD

**Expected Monthly Volume:**
- First 6 months: $20,000 - $50,000 USD
- Projected 12 months: $100,000+ USD
- 15-25 transactions per month initially

**Products/Services:**
1. Original seal engravings (篆刻)
2. Calligraphy works (书法)
3. Yixing zisha teapots and ceramics (紫砂陶瓷)
4. Contemporary ink paintings (水墨画)
5. Curated art collections and thematic exhibitions

**Delivery Method:**
- Physical artworks shipped via insured courier (DHL/FedEx)
- Digital certificates of authenticity
- Provenance documentation
- Professional art packaging and handling

**Refund/Return Policy:**
- 14-day return window for undamaged works
- Authenticity guarantee with full refund if proven otherwise
- No returns on auction purchases or custom commissions

## 5. 常见问题准备

**Q: 为什么艺术品价格这么高？**
A: All works are original pieces by established Chinese artists with proven exhibition history. We provide certificates of authenticity and provenance documentation.

**Q: 如何处理运输损坏？**
A: All shipments are fully insured. We use professional art handlers and museum-grade packaging.

**Q: 如何验证艺术家身份？**
A: We conduct thorough due diligence including studio visits, exhibition history verification, and collector references.

**Q: 是否支持分期付款？**
A: Yes, we offer installment options for works over $2,000 through Stripe's partnership with Klarna/Afterpay.
